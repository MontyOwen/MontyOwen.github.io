Welcome

I'm Monty and I have a diverse skillset that can be applied across a spectrum of technical disciplines from design to code. After running an e-commerce business for 2 years, I realised I had a strong aptitude for creative and logical thinking. I set about making my skills commercialisable, gaining a qualification in UX design from Google. My case studies can be found here. Projects range from a navigational solution for the National Gallery, to a healthcare platform which centralises health related data to help gain greater meaning and understanding of it. Following this I began working with Troopr, a startup which helps the armed forces community by centralising available help from across the UK on one platform. Whilst working at Troopr I won a scholarship to study full-stack development at Nuyuw tech academy, further merging my problem solving skills and creativity. I'm now looking for a position as a web developer where I can apply these skills.
