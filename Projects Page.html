Ai1

Scope: UX | Conceptual
Timeline: 2 months

Ai1 was a project I undertook following the combination of the mass adoption of health wearables, which in turn generated huge volumes of data for users. After conducting qualitative and quantitative research across the user base it became clear users didn't know how to leverage the data generated by their wearables, which Ai1 looked to solve.
